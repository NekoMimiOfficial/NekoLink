# NekoLink  
Federated sns platform with a direct social feed and dms/circles/groups for chats  
This platform is a mashup of both Discord and Bluesky but erm... better :3

The UI looks very close to discord with a sidebar containing from top to bottom: Home page, social feed, DMs, Groups, Circles, add group, add circle  

There is a floating nav at the bottom left corner containing the current account and some quick actions plus the settings button  

Circles are the equivalent of Discord servers, contains text channels only, rudamentary role and perms system plus an invite only system with disposable invite links  

The social feed is basically Bluesky... that's it  
The social feed can also be viewed in picture-only mode, we dont support videos (at least initially)  

The NekoLink instance has it's own global emojis and badges created by the admins and badges assigneable exclusively by admins or if public by users  
Badges are like discord tags but they dont have a character limit and also have a colored background which can also be a gradient  

the idea is simple, the execution is long :'3
